# Employee Onboarding Checklist

New employees at TechCorp must complete the following steps within their first week:
1. Activate your corporate email account through the identity portal.
2. Enroll in multi-factor authentication using the TechCorp Authenticator app.
3. Complete the mandatory security awareness training in the LMS portal.
4. Request access to team-specific Slack channels through your manager.
5. Set up your development environment following the Engineering Wiki guide.

Your onboarding buddy will be assigned on day one. Reach out to People Operations if you have not received your welcome packet by end of day two.
